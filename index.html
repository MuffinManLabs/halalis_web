Our Story

The new Main Street late-night lifesaver.

Halali's is a modern fast-casual spot on Main Street, Richmond — built around customizable bowls, wraps, and crepes made with fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Pick your protein, stack your toppings, drown it in our house sauces, or lock in on a signature like the chopped cheese, chicken shawarma, or a loaded Super Bowl.

We're proud to be 100% halal, open 11am – 2am every day, and prouder still to feed an RVA crowd that's already made us feel like home.

House Made Daily

The signature three.

Three sauces. Blended fresh every morning. Always order all three — that's the rule.

White Sauce

Cool, creamy, garlicky. The one you'll keep dipping fries into long after the bowl is gone.

Halali's Sauce

The house signature. Smoky, deep, a little sweet — the reason the chopped cheese hits so hard.

Jalapeño-Lime

Bright cilantro, fresh jalapeño, real lime. The rebel of the trio. Heat with backbone.
